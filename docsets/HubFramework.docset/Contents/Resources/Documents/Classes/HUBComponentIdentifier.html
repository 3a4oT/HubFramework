HUBComponentIdentifier

@interface HUBComponentIdentifier : NSObject <NSCopying>

A component identifier consists of a namespace and a name, or just a name (with an inferred namespace). Identifiers can be constructed either programmatically or through a string.

The Hub Framework uses component identifiers to map a certain HUBComponentModel to a HUBComponent implementation.
